The population training¶

The population training is a whole mode of this project, totally focused on the training of a population from a first totally random generation to certain quantity of iteration of breeding and natural selection.

The AI training module.

Parameters:pop – the population
Returns:the game list

Parameters:

  • size – the current population queue size
Returns:

the completed list

  • scores – the scores of every players
